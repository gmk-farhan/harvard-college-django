INFRASTRUCTURE

LIBRARY


Library is well equipped with around 7982 books which include text books, reference books and EAMCET books on each subject. The College subscribes to many journals and magazines, which enrich the knowledge of the students. Audio-Visual support is also provided with TV, VCR and more than 100 video cassettes on subjects of curriculum.

LABORATORIES


There are four full-fledged, well-ventilated, spacious, fully equipped laboratories. Each laboratory can accommodate a batch of 40 students.

TRANSPORTATION


The College has a fleet of buses for students, plying from all important places of Hyderabad city.

GAMES & SPORTS


The College has a qualified Physical Director, who trains the students in all major games and sports with the help of fully equipped sports room. Besides, coaching is provided by experienced coaches in major games.
