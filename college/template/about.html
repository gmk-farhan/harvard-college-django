ABOUT THE COLLEGE


Brief Summary


The Harvard Junior College was established by the Harvard University in the year 1900 with a strength of 100 students. Harvard Junior college is situated in the heart of the city in the picturesque area of Begumpet. The Serene atmosphere with beautiful land-scapes keeps the students in pleasant mood. Congenial for learning process. The College offers instruction through English Medium and admits both boys and girls of age group 15-16 years in two year course of intermediate (separate classes for girls).

CURRICULUM OFFERED


Intermediate is a course of two years. The College offers instructions in 4 groups:

  • MPC (MATHEMETICS, PHYSICS & CHEMISTRY)
  • BiPC(BIOLOGY, PHYSICS & CHEMISTRY)
  • CEC (CIVICS, ECONOMICS & COMMERCE)
  • MEC (MATHEMETICS, ECONOMICS & COMMERCE)

Total intake capacity - 700 students

Second Languages offered: Arabic, Hindi, Urdu.

Separate sections for girls

OBJECTIVE


Intermediate is a very crucial period in the life of a student and attitude & performance at this level decides their future. The College strives very hard at training the students to achieve excellence in academic standards leading to their admission in professional and other courses.

RESULTS


The results of the junior college have been consistently very good (above 90%) with normally around 95% scoring Ist division reflecting the high standard of quality education.

FACULTY


The College has highly qualified and experienced lecturers as faculty members who are dedicated, imparting- value added education of high quality & making the students to realise their individual potential.
