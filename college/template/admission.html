ADMISSION

ELIGIBILTY


Students who have passed 10th class or equivalent examination of SSC/CBSC/ICSE or any other board recognized by the Board of Intermediate Education are eligible for admission. (eligibility certificate from BIE is required to be produced) by students who have passed from board & other than SSC. The College admits students who have passed the qualifying examination in first division (with 60% or more marks). Candidates with second division may be admitted subject to availability of seats.
